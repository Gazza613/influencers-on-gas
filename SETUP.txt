====================================================
  INFLUENCERS ON GAS — TEAM ACCESS GUIDE
====================================================

The app is live and hosted. Your team does NOT need to install
anything or log into Higgsfield or Claude — it's all set up
centrally on the GAS account.


HOW TO USE IT
--------------------------------------------------
1. Open Chrome and go to:

      https://influencers-on-gas.vercel.app

2. Enter the team password when prompted.
   (Ask Gary for the password if you don't have it yet.)

3. That's it — start creating influencers, photos, and videos.
   Image and video generation run on the GAS account automatically.


GOOD TO KNOW
--------------------------------------------------
- One password gets you in. You won't log into anything else.
- Your work (influencers, brand deals, boards) is saved in your
  browser on your device.
- Clearing your browser data resets what you see on that device.
- Access requests / questions:  grow@gasmarketing.co.za
